    About Alectro

    Alectro is a carbon footprint calculation software founded in 2019 and is now supporting 100+ clients within all industries.

    We are a team of climate-engaged individuals working together with the goal of enabling companies to measure their carbon footprint, act to reduce their impact and engage their ecosystem (employees, clients, suppliers) around their climate strategy.

    Our platform simplifies data collection and analysis through seamless integrations, ensuring carbon emissions calculations are precise, user-friendly, and cost-effective

    Join us in creating a growing community of climate-engaged organisations.
