Onboarding Guide: Paper and Printing

Getting Started

You have been selected as the data holder for Paper and Printing in the office. As such, we need you to complete the template we have provided.

This guide will help you to complete the data into the format needed to build this part of the company impact.

Finding and Requesting Data

Printing is typically handled by an external company and billed by the page. That makes it easy to get detailed and accurate information on how much you print.

In shared offices, you may also have paper printed from communal machines. In this case, you can ask the Office Manager and they'll be able to run a report for you.

Template If you need to request the data you can use the following email template. Simply copy and paste into your email client and send to the supplier account manager.

Dear [data holder],
We're undertaking an analysis of our carbon impact and need some information about our paper and printing.
If you could provide me the following information for the period of [start date to end date], I'd be grateful:

Printed Pages
  • Usage: The total number of pages printed per month for each printer.
Many thanks, and please let me know if you have any questions!


Completing Data Template

The Paper and Printing Template

Admin Data

The orange panel on the left hand side of the sheet gives you all your admin data.

Admin Data

  • Basic Data: This is the company name and the period of data analysis
  • Location Data: This is the location of offices to complete data input for

Progress Data

  • Progress: here you can track the progress of the data collection
  • Completed By: Once this data has been completed, add your email and completion data so that we can ask any questions upon verfication.


Paper and Printing Data

The locations and addresses will copy over automatically into the Office Location column. All you need to do is fill in the usage data and the supplementary data associated with it.

For each item type, enter the number of pages printed during each month in question, for each office location.

For each type, you can also enter the Main Supplier if known. This gives us information on the status of your Environmentally Preferred Purchasing strategy.

Data is typically given in the following four categories:

  • A4 Mono: A4 black and white
  • A4 Colour: A4 printed in colour
  • A3 Mono: A3 black and white
  • A3 Colour: A3 printed in colour

The good news is that Paper and Printing information is one of best documented pieces of information in the project, so you should be able to get the infomation very easily!

Frequently Asked Questions


What if we're in a WeWork or similar?

The office manager will be able to give you a usage report from their systems.


What if the data isn't delivered in the format as requested?

Typically it won't stray far from this format. You should be able to make it work with this format, but get in touch with any questions and we'll help out.


What if printers are at locations other than our offices?

Sometimes we see Directors in companies who have a company printer at home. In this instance, simply add this printers output to the primary location the employee is based at.
For example, if Employee A has a printer at their home (Location X), but their primary location is Location A, then add Location X to Location A's running total.
