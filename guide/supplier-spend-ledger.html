Onboarding Guide: Supplier Spend Ledger

Getting Started

You have been selected as the data holder for Supplier Spend Ledger. As such, we need you to upload the requested data so that we can analyse the data.

This guide will help you to complete the data into the format needed to build this part of the company impact.

Finding and Requesting Data

Typically this data should be available as a direct download or export from an expense software.

Please complete this through a direct .xls extraction from your CRM or Expense System.

Data should be provided as the sum of the spend per supplier.

Completing Data Template


Admin Data

The orange panel on the left hand side of the sheet gives you all your admin data.

Admin Data

  • Basic Data: This is the company name and the period of data analysis

Progress Data

  • Progress: here you can track the progress of the data collection
  • Completed By: Once this data has been completed, add your email and completion data so that we can ask any questions upon verfication.


Expense Data

This guide will walk you through the information we require from the Supplier Spend Ledger

  • id: This can be left blank and it will be automtically created on upload
  • Supplier Name: The name of the supplier as listed in you CRM/Supplier Management System
  • Supplier Category (if exists): Your broad categorisation of the data
  • Supplier Category 2 (if exists): Any additional categorisation you use
  • Total Annual Supplier Spend (GBP): The total spend at that supplier during the period of analysis

Frequently Asked Questions


What if I don't know the data?

This data should be accessible for any organisation that has to do a set of annual accounts. Please get in touch with us if you think you will have trouble accessing the data.


What to do if I have individual suppliers?

When it comes to individual suppliers it can be really hard to measure the individual impact. You can select "External Consultant" in place of these actual supplier names, and we'll adjust the data calculations accordingly.
