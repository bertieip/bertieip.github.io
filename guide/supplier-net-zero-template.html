Completing the Data

Company type

Are you a micro business?

Companies of less than 10 employees are referred to a micro businesses. Generally, they are under different reporting rules than SMEs and so reporting emissions is rarer. If you're a micro business of under 10 employees then please answer Yes and return the form to your client.

Baseline Measurement

This section refers to the first year of emissions reporting - referred to as the Baseline year. It is the basis from which emission reductions are measured.

Current Reporting Period

This is data associated with the current, or most recent, reporting period. If this is the first year of reporting, then data will be the same as in the Baseline Emissions.

Information requested

  • Scope 1: these emissions refer to direct greenhouse gas emissions produced by an organization's own activities or operations, such as emissions from on-site combustion of fossil fuels or company-owned vehicles.
  • Scope 2: these emissions are indirect greenhouse gas emissions associated with the generation of electricity, heat, or steam that an organization purchases or consumes. Market-based emissions account for the greenhouse gases associated with purchased energy using emission factors reflecting the environmental attributes of that energy, while location-based emissions rely on the average emissions intensity of the energy supply in a specific geographical location.
  • Scope 3: these emissions encompass all indirect greenhouse gas emissions that result from an organization's activities but occur from sources not owned or controlled by the organization, such as emissions from the supply chain, employee commuting, or customer product use.
  • Full time Employees: The average count of employees during this reporting period
  • Emissions Intensity: This tCO2e / FTE value will calculate automatically, but can be added mannually if this is different from the straight division of 'Total emissions' / 'Full time employees'.

Reporting Categories

Provide information on which GHG Protocol categories are provided, especially for Scope 3 emissions. Guidance on each category can be found on the GHG Protocol website: https://ghgprotocol.org/

Revenue

Responsible Procurement

Please provide data about the responsible procurement habits of the business

Modern Slavery

Modern Slavery Due Diligence is a critical process that organizations undertake to identify, prevent, and address instances of forced labor and human trafficking within their supply chains and operations, ensuring compliance with ethical and legal standards.

Environmentally Preferred Procurement

Environmentally Preferred Procurement prioritises the selection of services with lower environmental impacts, helping organizations reduce their footprint and promote sustainability.



Frequently Asked Questions


What if I don't know the data?

If any data is unknown, it can be left blank but the client will see this as incomplete data.

Can I get assistance in completing this form?

The data should be available if the company has completed the emission measurement steps previously. To get help in measuring emissions you can contact alectro by emailing them.
