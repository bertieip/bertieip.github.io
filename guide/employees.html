Onboarding Guide: Human Resources

You have been selected as the Human Resource data holder. As such, we need you to complete the wizard for in the Reporting Period.

You'll see the following task in your to-do list here: vso.alectro.io/todo-list

This guide will help you to complete the data into the format needed to build this part of the company impact.

Finding and Requesting Data

As most information is internal, you should be able to complete the template from your own systems.

We will be asking for a list of employees for the period in question, and current employees for ongoing analysis.

For example, if the period of analysis is 01/01/2022 - 31/12/2022 then please add any employees who have worked any period of time at the company from 01/01/2022 to today.

We will not contact anyone who is no longer at the company.

We have provided a template that you can download to collect the data before uploading it if required.

Click here to download the template

Completing the Data Upload

When you click to begin your task, you'll see the Employees onboarding panel.

Step 1: Complete the Basic Information

Click Complete Template and Upload to begin

You can download the template by clicking the Download button.

The Location ID is important as it corresponds to where the employee is affiliated to. You'll need the numbers when you fill in the template.

Once you download and open the template, you'll see a basic .csv file to populate. You'll need to complete the following fields for each employee:

  • Email (email): This is the employee's company email address. We will use this to allow them to login to the platform, and enter their own data for onboarding.
  • First Name (first_name): The employee name is used to personalise the platform. They can change this later in the process.
  • Last Name (last_name): The employee name is used to personalise the platform. They can change this later in the process.
  • Start Date (during_yyyy-mm-dd): Enter the date that the employee joined the company. If the employee joined the company before the period of analysis, and the start date is unknown, then use the start of the analysis period. For example, if the period of analysis is 01/01/2022 - 31/12/2022 then please use 01/01/2022 as the start date.
  • Primary Location (primary_location_id): Although many employees have flexibility, please assign a primary location for each employee. This is taken from the Location ID on the previous step.
  • Employee Full Time Equivalent (FTE) (employee_fte): TO account for full time and part time employees, we need to assign an employee_fte value. 1 means they work full time, and is based on a 40 hour week. Please assign this value. For a simple calculation, 0.5 would be equivalent to working 20 hours a week, or a four day week would be 0.8.

When this is completed, save the document and return to the onboarding wizard.

Click Next on the Download Template modal, and then click to select the file of the completed template.

Click Upload Employees, and you'll be taken to the next page.

Step 2: Tweak your data

Now you've completed the initial upload, you can tweak the data for each individual employee.

Once you've made your edits, check the box on the right hand side, and click to 'Confirm Employees'.

Your confirmation message will show how many employees you're adding, and by clicking Confirm, you'll send them the Welcome email so they can complete their onboarding.

Step 3: Mark as Complete

Once you've added the users, you can return to your onboarding wizard by clicking to Go Back. You can also see employees who've been added by clicking the Show Completed toggle.

Back the onboarding wizard homepage and you can mark the whole section as complete by clicking Mark as Complete

The section will complete, and you can go back to your to-do list if you have other items to complete, else your work is done!


Frequently Asked Questions


What if I don't know the data?

Please use data as accurate as possible to complete this. Email is the most important component of the analysis, as we can then ask individuals to complete any information that is missing.

When adding dates, the month is more important than the day, so the 1st or last day of the month is sufficient in most cases.
