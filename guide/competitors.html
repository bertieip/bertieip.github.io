Onboarding Guide: Competitors

Getting Started

You have been selected as the data holder for Competitors. As such, we need you to complete the competitors wizard to tell us who your competitors are.

You'll see the following task in your to-do list here: vso.alectro.io/todo-list

This guide will help you to enter the correct data to build this part of the company's impact.

Finding and Requesting Data

Competitors are simply the companies that you compare yourself against, or would consider to work in a similar space. Even if they're a huge organisation, you can still compare against what they're doing with the ESG strategy.

For example, if you're a neobank you can still compare against a large bank like NatWest - we'll simply give you the information you need to unserstand what these competitors are doing at the moment to allow you to stay up to date.

As this is purely a subjective piece of data, you can enter this data as soon as you're happy with your choice.


Completing the Data

Using the Onboarding Wizard

When you click to begin your task, you'll see the Competitors onboarding panel.

Step 1: Start Wizard

Click Start Wizard to enter the data

Step 2: Find Existing Competitor, or Create a new One

Start typing to see if your competitor already exists in the database.

If your competitor exists
  • Select the competitor, and click Next to move to the next page.
  • Submit your choice
Add a new competitor

If you cannot find your competitor, then click the '+ Create New Competitor' button.

On the next screen you can enter the following information:

  • Competitor Name: Add the name of the competitor here
  • Industry Type: Start typing to find the SIC code of the competitor. Companies House will give you this data if you're unsure.
  • Competitor Website: Paste the company URL here

Click submit to send the data for analysis.

Step 3: Competitor Status

The competitor will mvoe from 'Analysis Underway' to 'Competitor Added' when the analysis has been completed by the Alectro team.

Step 4: Mark as Complete

You'll see a list of competitors that have been created in the wizard homepage. Once you're satisfied you've added all of your competitors, you can mark this data as complete.

The section will complete, and you can go back to your to-do list if you have other items to complete, else your work is done!

Your to-do list will now have a completed message in it.
