Getting Started

The magic links in our onboarding emails expire after a defined period of time, so we encourage users to complete their onboarding as soon as they recieve the welcome email.

If the user tries to do this after the expiration, they can request a password reset link to begin the process.

Follow the steps below to login to the platform if this happens.
