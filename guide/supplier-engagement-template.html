Baseline Measurement

This section refers to the first year of emissions reporting - referred to as the Baseline year. It is the basis from which emission reductions are measured.

Current Reporting Period

This is data associated with the current, or most recent, reporting period. If this is the first year of reporting, then data will be the same as in the Baseline Emissions.

Emissions Attribution

If you can attribute your own emissions directly to an individual client, enter the relevant details here. This allows your client to report Scope 3 emissions with a greater level of accuracy. This can be calculated as % of revenue allocation if the direct contribution is unknown.

Modern Slavery

Modern Slavery Due Diligence is a critical process that organizations undertake to identify, prevent, and address instances of forced labor and human trafficking within their supply chains and operations, ensuring compliance with ethical and legal standards.

Environmentally Preferred Procurement

Environmentally Preferred Procurement prioritises the selection of services with lower environmental impacts, helping organizations reduce their footprint and promote sustainability.



Frequently Asked Questions


What if I don't know the data?

If any data is unknown, it can be left blank but the client will see this as incomplete data.

Can I get assistance in completing this form?

The data should be available, if the company has completed the emission measurement steps previously. To get help in measuring emissions you can contact Alectro by emailing them.
