Onboarding Guide: Company Vehicles

Getting Started

You have been selected as the data holder for Company Vehicles and Company Mileage. As such, we need you to complete the template we have provided.

This guide will help you to complete the data into the format needed to build this part of the company impact.

Finding and Requesting Data

By Company Vehicles and Company Mileage, we are referring to either:

  1. Vehicles driven for operational purposes: For example, a delivery vehicle or a response vehicle
  2. Vehicles owned by the company and provided to employees for work and personal use: For example, traditional company car used for sales and also personal use

We will use this data to calculate direct combusion from vehicles (Scope 1) and also the embodied emissions of the vehicle (Scope 3).

Mileage in personal cars will be calculated seperately and included in the Business Travel category (Scope 3). Personal vehicles do not have embodied emissions calculated as part of the company impact.

As most of this data is internal, there should be no need to request external data.


Completing Data Template

The Operational Vehicles Template

Admin Data

The orange panel on the left hand side of the sheet gives you all your admin data.

Admin Data

  • Basic Data: This is the company name and the period of data analysis

Progress Data

  • Progress: here you can track the progress of the data collection
  • Completed By: Once this data has been completed, add your email and completion data so that we can ask any questions upon verfication.


Operational Data

All you need to do is fill in the yellow boxes, providing any supplementary data associated with it, and we'll do the rest.

For each Vehicle there is a Vehicle Type section and a Vehicle Distance section. We've added space for up to 30 vehicles, but you can add more as required:

Vehicle Type Data
  • ID (e.g reg): this is simply an identifying field and allows you to track the individual vehicle
  • Type: this is the type of vehicle with options for Car, Motorcycle or Van
  • Make: this is the make of the vehicle, for example Ford or VW
  • Model: this is the model of the vehicle, for example Focus (for Ford) or Golf (for VW)
  • Fuel Type: this is the fuel type of the vehicle with options for Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) or Plug-In Hybrid (PHEV). The difference between Hydrid, PHEV, and EV is significant so please ensure these are distinguished.
  • Start Date: This is the purchase/lease start date for the vehicle.
  • End Date: This is the purchase/lease end date for the vehicle if it occured during the period of analysis. Leave blank if a current asset.
Vehicle Distance Data
  • Monthly/Annual: Allows you to input data on an annual basis if unavailable during the year.
  • Year Total: If you select Annual above, then input the total distance travelled in kilometers (km) during the year. This will then populate the data automatically on a pro-rata basis monthly.
  • Monthly Total: If you have distance data on a monthly basis then you can enter this in directly. This gives the most accurate calculation of carbon impact and will allow you to analyse why some months had a bigger impact than others.

Frequently Asked Questions


Does a lease car include a hire car?

No. This section of the analysis focusses on vehicles which are, for all intents-and-purposes, a full time company vehicle. Hire cars and vans will be included in expense data and Business Travel (Scope 3)
