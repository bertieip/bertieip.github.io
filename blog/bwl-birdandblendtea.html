    We caught up with Krisi Smith, Co-Founder & Creative Director at Bird & Blend Tea Co., to discuss their journey and the future.

    Why does Bird & Blend Tea Co. exist?

    "Myself and my co-founder Mike set up the company 10 years ago and from day 1, we had sustainability high up on our priority list and putting people & planet above profit was incorporated into our company values.

    As we‚Äôve grown over the years, our commitment to our sustainability goals continues to remain at the forefront of our decision making. Trying to be an eco-friendly tea company is a big part of why Bird & Blend exists and we pledge to be more environmentally friendly each year.

    For example, our packaging is 100% compostable and plastic free, we are carbon neutral and we are now B Corp accredited."

    What are the key things that you do best?

    What are the trends in your market space?

    "Some of the trends in the food & drink market include ready-to-drink and food-to-go solutions that taste great, are healthy, have a premium feel but are affordable.
