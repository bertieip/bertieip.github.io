What key things does Boundless do best?

Healthy products

Boundless uses activation to create its products, a method which involves soaking nuts and seeds in water. This process reduces levels of phytic acid which causes a bitter taste, whilst also increasing the bioavailability of nutrients and the nutritional value of grains.

Packaging

Currently Boundless is using 100% recyclable packaging. In the snacking world, this can be very difficult as more eco-friendly packaging means that snacks can often have a shorter shelf life compared to non-environmentally friendly packaging, such as plastics.

At the same time, it wants to integrate new technologies which could improve its products and materials.

Ethos

Honesty: being truthful to their customers, their work and their team who are all treated equally. Natural: making all products from natural ingredients using a natural process. Authenticity: staying true to their brand and what they stand for which is to make people aware of healthy snack alternatives.
