How-to Guide: Data Types

Getting Started

We've distilled data collection into three key data-types. This enables us to send succinct and direct data requests to the individuals in the company who have access to the right data at their fingertips.

The three data types are:

  1. Template Data: The simplest data type. Template data can be completed according to the templates we provide. The individuals we ask to complete this data will be close enough to the data type to access it quickly. This includes office food and drink, electricity and other utilities, and IT equipment.
  2. Employee Data: Each employee completes a simple 5 minute survey to give information that would otherwise be almost impossible to predict at a company level.
  3. Expense Data: The most complicated data to access and analyse, our text-based algoritm approach gives SMEs more granular information that any other spend based method could. Our reconciliation tool makes it simple to input data as required and will continue to be improved with machine learning improvements.
