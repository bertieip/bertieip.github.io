    Case Study: Moneybox

    Moneybox is a UK-based app that aims to make investing easy and accessible for everyone. Users can round up their everyday purchases and invest the spare change into a range of investment options.

    What the problem was?
    • To understand the impact of their operations and services
    • To identify opportunities to reduce their carbon emissions and operate more sustainably
    • To meet the increasing demands from consumers and investors for transparency on environmental performance
    • To align with global efforts to mitigate climate change and contribute to a low-carbon economy.
    How did we help?
    • Implementing remote work policies to reduce employee travel and commuting expenses
    • By publicly reporting their carbon emissions, Moneybox was able to demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and attract environmentally-conscious customers and investors
    • Implementing energy-efficient measures in their offices and data centers helped Moneybox reduce their energy consumption and lower their utility bills.
